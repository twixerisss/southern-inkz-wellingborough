01 Tattoos All sizes · consultation only +

Every piece at Southern Inkz starts with a consultation — no matter the size. We sit down, plan the design and placement properly, then give you a real quote based on size, detail and time. Nothing rushed, nothing guessed.

  • Mini & single-session pieces Small, fine line, script, hidden marks — perfect for a first tattoo or a subtle add-on.
  • Custom designs Drawn from scratch to your brief — bring a reference, a feeling, or just a vibe.
  • Cover-ups Old or unwanted ink turned into something you'll actually want to wear.
  • Full sleeves & large pieces Designed cohesively, executed across multiple sessions with proper planning.
  • Style Fine line · realism · traditional · Japanese · whatever the piece calls for.

Final quote at consultation · £20 deposit secures your booking · comes off the final price.
