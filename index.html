About the Studio

Southern Inkz is Wellingborough's go-to tattoo and piercing studio, located on Queen Street. Our experienced artists specialise in every style from fine line and realism to traditional and Japanese. We pride ourselves on creating a welcoming, clean, and sterile environment where your ideas come to life in ink. Whether it's your first tattoo or your fiftieth, we treat every piece with the same care and attention. Walk-ins are welcome, but booking a consultation ensures dedicated time for your vision.
